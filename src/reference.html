Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about booking through Aperture.

How does the AI matching work?
Our algorithm analyzes your event type, preferred photography style, budget range, and location to match you with photographers who specialize in your type of event and align with your aesthetic preferences.
Can I request specific photographers?
Absolutely! If you have a particular photographer in mind, you can search for them by name or browse our directory of professionals. Our matching system will still help you discover similar photographers you might like.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
We offer flexible rescheduling options based on photographer availability. Cancellation policies vary by photographer, but we clearly display each professional's policy before you book.
How long until I receive my photos?
Most photographers deliver edited photos within 2-3 weeks of your event. You'll receive a notification as soon as your photos are ready in your personal online gallery.
What if I'm not happy with the photos?
We have a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your photos, we'll work with you and the photographer to address your concerns, which may include additional editing or in rare cases, a partial refund.
